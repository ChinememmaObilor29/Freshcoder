Welcome to hanbil technologies

Hanbil Technologies Limited provides comprehensive integrated technology services for the Nigerian Oil and Gas Industry. Thanks to its concept of an Oil & Gas Service Center, hanbil is nowadays widely recognized all over the world as benchmark for oil companies operating in West Africa. Corporate Vision and Objectives Hanbil technologies Ltd.’s vision is to partner with governments and clients to proactively offer tailor-made logistic solutions and state of the art infrastructure to the Oil and Gas Industry. Leading the market as a core infrastructure developer and services provider, through its skill, efficiency, integrity and quality of service.




Hanbil Technologies Policy


In support of the Nigerian Content Policy of the Federal Government of Nigeria, Hanbil Technologies Limited is committed to maximizing in a sustainable manner the use of Nigerian human resources, materials, equipment and services, without compromising the company’s values or quality, health, safety and environmental standards. In order to contribute to the development of Nigeria’s industrial capabilities and competences and supporting the nation’s economy, Hanbil shall seek to maximize the participation of Nigerian businesses and local contractors in its operations in compliance with the Nigerian Oil and Gas Industry Content Development Act, 2010. Hanbil shall take all reasonable measures to meet requisite Nigerian Content Plan in projects supported by its facilities and services without compromising the intended objective of achieving the best overall value for the projects in terms of price, quality, efficiency, delivery and operating parameters.
